about.me

Hey there! I'm Nate — a coding hobbyist who loves tinkering with technology and building projects to solve problems. Started learning to code a while back and got hooked on the feeling of turning ideas into working software.

I spend my free time experimenting with new frameworks, contributing to open source when I can, and working on side projects that catch my interest. Most of my work is learning by doing – the best bugs are the ones that teach you something new!

This site is basically my digital playground where I showcase the stuff I've built. Some projects are finished, some are works-in-progress, and some are just fun experiments. Feel free to check out my code or reach out if you want to chat about tech!
